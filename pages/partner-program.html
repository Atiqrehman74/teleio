FAQ

Common Questions

Is it free to join the Partner Program? ▼
Yes — completely free. There are no joining fees, monthly fees, or hidden charges. You sign up and start earning immediately.
How are commissions calculated? ▼
Commission is a percentage of the visa/service fee paid by your referred client. Your tier determines the rate (Bronze 8%, Silver 10%, Gold 12%, Platinum 15%). You move tiers automatically as your referral count grows.
When and how do I get paid? ▼
Payouts are processed monthly (Bronze), bi-weekly (Silver), weekly (Gold), or daily (Platinum). We support bank transfers and popular e-wallets in the UAE and internationally.
What countries does Teleio cover? ▼
We process visas for 70+ countries including UK, USA, Schengen Area (26 countries), UAE, Canada, Australia, Turkey, Malaysia, Thailand, and more.
Can I track how many people used my referral link? ▼
Yes. Your partner dashboard shows every referral click, application submitted, booking completed, and commission earned — all in real time.
Can I add sub-agents under my account? ▼
Gold and Platinum partners can manage a team of sub-agents. Their referrals count toward your tier progression and you earn an override commission on their bookings.
