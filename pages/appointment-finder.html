Tips for Booking Appointments

Book Early

Embassy appointments can be booked out weeks in advance. Book as early as possible.

Prepare Documents

Have all documents ready before your appointment date to avoid rescheduling.

Confirmation

You’ll receive an email and SMS confirmation after booking. Save your reference number.

Rescheduling

Most appointments can be rescheduled up to 24 hours before, subject to availability.
