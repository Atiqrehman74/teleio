Photo Requirements Guide

📏
Size Requirements

35×45mm (standard) · Printed at 300 DPI minimum

⬜
Background

Plain white or off-white background · No patterns or shadows

👤
Face Position

Looking directly at camera · Centered in frame · 70-80% of frame height

👁️
Eyes & Expression

Eyes open and clearly visible · Neutral expression · Mouth closed

🕶️
Glasses

No glasses (most countries require this for new applications)

💡
Lighting

Evenly lit · No shadows on face or background · No red-eye
