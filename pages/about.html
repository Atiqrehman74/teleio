Our Mission is to Make
Travel Borderless

Founded in Dubai in 2020, Teleio Tourism was built on a simple belief: getting a visa or booking a trip should never be the hardest part of traveling. Bureaucracy, paperwork, and uncertainty shouldn't stand between people and the experiences they dream of.

We combine AI-powered automation with deep human expertise to process visa applications for 70+ countries — faster, more accurately, and with full transparency at every step.

Today we serve individual travelers, families, corporate clients, and 2,000+ travel agency partners across the UAE and beyond.
