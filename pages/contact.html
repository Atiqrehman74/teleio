Quick Answers

How long does visa processing take?

Tourist visas are typically processed in 2–5 business days. Express processing is available for most countries in 24 hours.

Can I visit your office without an appointment?

Yes — walk-ins are welcome during office hours (Mon–Fri 9am–7pm, Sat 10am–5pm). For complex cases, we recommend booking a slot.

Do you have parking at The Prism Tower?

Yes, visitor parking is available in the building basement. The tower is also a 5-minute walk from Business Bay Metro Station.

Can I get support in Arabic or Urdu?

Absolutely. Our team speaks English, Arabic, Urdu, Hindi, and Filipino. Call or WhatsApp us and we'll connect you with the right agent.
