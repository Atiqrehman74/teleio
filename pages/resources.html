Frequently Asked Questions

How long does visa processing take?

▾

Processing times vary by country. Standard tourist visas typically take 5–15 business days. eVisas can be processed in 24–72 hours. We recommend applying at least 4–6 weeks before travel.

What documents are required for a tourist visa?

▾

Typically: valid passport, passport photos, bank statements, travel itinerary, accommodation proof, and application form. Requirements vary by destination country.

Can I apply for a visa without a travel itinerary?

▾

Most embassies require a travel itinerary. You can use a provisional booking — you don’t need to purchase tickets before visa approval for most destinations.

What is a Schengen visa?

▾

A Schengen visa allows you to travel freely across 27 European countries (the Schengen Area) with a single visa. It’s valid for up to 90 days within a 180-day period.
